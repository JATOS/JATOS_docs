Easy to Use

JATOS was designed from the ground up to be easily installed and used to get your experiment set-up online and run quickly.

Focus on What Matters

JATOS lets you focus on your experiment, and we'll do the chores. Moreover, you can host it on any server (at your University or in the cloud). Go ahead and move your experiment into the Example Studies directory.
